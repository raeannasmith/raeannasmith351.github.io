My Current Workout Routines

I separate my workouts by upper and lower body weightlifting days, and one day designated for cardio. I prefer structured routines, so my workouts look very similar every week.

Lower Body Days: Monday, Wednesday, Friday

  1. Barbell Squats: 4 sets of 8
  2. Hip Thrusts: 4 sets of 10
  3. Romanian Dead Lifts: 4 sets of 8
  4. Cable Kickbacks: 4 sets of 12 per leg
  5. Hip Abductors: 4 sets of 12
  6. Leg Extensions: 4 sets of 8
  7. Finish with 20 minutes of cardio

Upper Body Days: Tuesday and Thursday

  1. Cable Rows: 4 sets of 8
  2. Lateral Pull Downs: 4 sets of 8
  3. Shoulder Press: 4 sets of 8
  4. Bicep Curls: 4 sets of 8
  5. Skull Crushers: 4 sets of 8
  6. Finish with 20 minutes of cardio

Cardio and Core Day: Sunday

  1. 45 minutes of cardio
  2. Crunches: 100
  3. Leg Lifts: 50
  4. Sit Ups: 50
  5. Reverse Crunches: 50
  6. Scissor Kicks: 100
