How I Stay Motivated

Staying active is definitely a challenge! I stay motivated by utilizing fitness and movement as a way to destress from my school work. If I am feeling overwhelmed, its easy to go on a walk or use my workouts as a study break.

Consistency is the most important aspect if you are focused on achieving specific results. I stay consistent by following a routine. For example, I find that I workout better in the morning. Therefore, I workout everyday before work or class at around 6 a.m. and do the same type of workout depending on the day of the week. This eliminates any additional stress of trying to determine which workout to do everyday.

Working out does not have to be a negative activity! If you do not love working out, try to find ways to make it more enjoyable. I workout with friends! This makes exercising feel more like a social activity, and makes time go by faster.
