About Me

Hello! My name is RaeAnna Smith and I am currently a sophomore at UW-Madison. I have always been an active person and played many sports when I was younger. In high school, I was on my school's dance team and loved every second of it. When I got to college, I knew I wanted to stay active somehow. Luckily, UW-Madison has amazing facilities that are easily accessible. Beginning in my freshman year, I made personal goals to go to the gym a few times a week. Once I got more comfortable in the gym, I fell in love with weight training and cardio. From there, my fitness journey blossomed.

My Current Goals

As a busy college student, I prioritize balance, but hold my fitness and health to a high standard. I am currently working out 6 days a week for one hour and incorporating one active rest day for balance and recovery. My goals also include drinking a gallon of water daily, and prioritizing proteins and fibers in my diet while remaining in a calorie deficit. I choose not to focus on weight, and instead focus on how my body feels!
