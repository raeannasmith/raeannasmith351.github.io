Healthy Meals For College Students

Eating healthy is hard, especially on a tight budget and little time. When planning my meals, I prioritze protein for muscle synthesis and recovery. I also try to watch my calorie intake, but try not to monitor it too exessively. Here are a few of my go to meals:

Burger Bowl
A bowl of ground beef and any burger toppings. I use lettuce, pickles, cheese and avocado in mine.
Yogurt Bowl
Non-fat plain greek yogurt with protein powder, peanut butter, honey, etc.
Chicken and ... anything!
Frozen chicken breast cooked in the air fryer paired with any frozen vegetables. I prefer broccoli.
